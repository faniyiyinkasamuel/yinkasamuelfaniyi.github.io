ABOUT ME

Hi, I am Yinka Samuel Faniyi, a results-driven Data Analyst with a strong background in business intelligence, data visualization, and operational efficiency. I specialize in turning raw data into actionable insights that help organizations improve performance, streamline processes, and make data-informed decisions. My journey into data analytics is built on a solid foundation of technical systems support, workflow optimization, and administrative process automation. While supporting employees and senior executives in fast-paced environments, I routinely designed and implemented solutions that reduced downtime, improved reporting accuracy, and enhanced operational decision-making. These experiences sharpened my ability to identify bottlenecks, leverage technology, and drive process improvements, all of which now inform my analytical work. With tools like Power BI, SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Microsoft Fabric, I build clear, data-driven narratives and dashboards that align with business goals. My approach blends analytical rigor with problem-solving and data storytelling to guide strategic decision-making across teams.
